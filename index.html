Steppe Voices

This project is dedicated to translating, preserving, and sharing Kazakh literary and historical works. All materials are not for redistribution. For academic and personal access only. Contact: aamanta1@binghamton.edu

项目简介

Steppe Voices 是一个致力于哈萨克文学翻译与传播的平台。我们希望通过中英文对照的方式， 让更多人了解哈萨克人民丰富的文化遗产。

Project Introduction

Steppe Voices is a platform dedicated to the translation and dissemination of Kazakh literature. We aim to bring the cultural heritage of the Kazakh people to a broader audience through bilingual editions.
