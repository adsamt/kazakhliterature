Steppe Voices

项目简介：

这是一个致力于翻译和保存哈萨克文学的三语平台，使用哈萨克语、中文和英文三种语言。该项目旨在帮助研究哈萨克文化的学者和不会母语的中文使用者，了解丰富的哈萨克文学与历史传统。

译者介绍：

艾多斯·阿曼泰，1989年生，成长于北京，哈萨克国立大学本科毕业，现就读于纽约州立大学宾汉姆顿分校历史系博士项目，研究方向为中亚历史与哈萨克文化。

Project Introduction:

This is a trilingual platform dedicated to translating and preserving Kazakh literature, presented in Kazakh, Chinese, and English. It aims to help scholars and Chinese-speaking audiences understand the richness of Kazakh literary and historical traditions.

Translator Introduction:

Aidos Amantai, born in 1989, raised in Beijing, graduated from the Kazakh National University. Currently pursuing a PhD in History at Binghamton University, SUNY, focusing on Central Asian history and Kazakh culture.
